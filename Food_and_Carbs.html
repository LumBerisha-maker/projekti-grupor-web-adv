Rice and Meat

Rice is a cereal grain and in its domesticated form is the staple food of over half of the world's population, particularly in Asia and Africa. Rice is the seed of the grass species Oryza sativa (Asian rice)

Oats and berries

The combination of oats and berries has been a staple in many health-conscious individuals’ diets for years. The idea of pairing the fiber-rich, comforting goodness of oats with the antioxidant-packed, sweet flavor of berries seems like a match made in heaven. But is this pairing truly beneficial for our health, or are there some underlying concerns that we should be aware of?

Milkshakes

People often associate protein milkshakes with a less-than-desirable texture and taste. However, that doesn’t have to be the case. With a little creativity and experimentation in the kitchen, it’s possible to create rich, smooth, and indulgent protein milkshakes that are perfect for fueling up after a workout or as a quick snack on-the-go. These shakes can be just as decadent and sweet as your favorite ice cream-filled treats, minus the added calories and guilt.
